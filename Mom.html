My mom, Meseret

Who is my mom?

My mom is from the capital of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa. She immigrated here 8 months after my dad, Henok. She first went to Chicago and Iowa. Then she went to Olney, where I was born. Then we settled just to the south of Olney, in Silver Spring, some 15 minutes away from there.

Sports and Recreation

My mom likes sports, but doesn't have time to watch them. She especially liked Kobe Bryant, who died a few years ago. (RIP Kobe.) My mom's favorite singer is Ephrem Tamiru, an Ethiopian singer.
